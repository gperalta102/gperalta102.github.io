Android Music Player

Play music with visualizer.

Music playing application for Android devices. Able to find music files on device and play the music files. Displays files information with a recycler view. Utilizes Android MediaPlayer class. Implements MVVM.

Project for my Mobile Application Development. The app utilizes fragments and a recycler view for the list of songs. The application gets the files from the shared storage on the device. After getting permission from the user to access the devices storage, I used a content resolver to get the audio files information like track name, artist, and uri.

When a user selects a song from the recycler view, it opens another fragment made to play the media. The song information and uri are passed along to the music player fragment which shows the song title and the artist with control for the music. When the user presses the play button a MediaPlayer instance is created and the uri is fed to it. When the MediaPlayer instance gets the uri it begins to play the song.

When the song is playing there is an open source visualizer which reacts to the music playing.
